About Me

Hi! I'm Mike — a software engineer, photographer and graphic/web designer.

You might have noticed that I take a lot of photographs of LEGO. My work has been published in national newspapers in the UK, seen on BBC television, and featured in many magazines all over the world with subjects as diverse as German political culture and Brazilian business news.

I was a regular participant in LEGOLAND California's Star Wars days and I've had my work exhibited all over the world.

On the design side I am very fond of designing logos, posters and design systems for print and digital. I've been doing this sort of thing  as a hobby for over ten years now. Always looking for excuses to wrangle some vectors.

I'm not very active on social networks, but you will find me on a few (links at the bottom of the page). My full photography portfolio is over on Flickr.
